ADVENTURE OF MY 1-HORN SHEEP

INSTRUCTIONS

- Use left / right arrow (← / →) to move sheep to left and right. The longer you go, the more Love Score you can get

- Avoid accident by dodging X-mas trees coming to you

- Sheep's Energy decrease by time, you need to touch Milktea on road to add 250~400 Energy for her. Don't let Energy drop down to Zero

- Sometimes Wolf will appear. Left-click on Wolf to earn 100 Love Score
